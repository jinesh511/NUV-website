Welcome to PhD Program at Navrachana University

Orientation Program is scheduled on : 11.00am, Saturday, 8 August 2020

The University offers academic programs leading to the award of PhD Degree through its Schools

  1. School of Science
  2. School of Business & Law
  3. School of Engineering & Technology
  4. School of Liberal Studies & Education

The University encourages research leading to PhD degree with focus on Interdisciplinary Research for the creation and addition of knowledge and to address complex scientific and socially relevant problems via integration of knowledge from multiple and disparate fields.

Application Process

For admission to PhD program (July 2020 Intake), candidates need to register and fill the application form ONLINE only between dates (1 June 30 June, 2020).

Last date of Online application: 30th June 2020

Announcement of list of eligible candidates: 2nd July 2020

Date of entrance exam: 11th July 2020 (Written test:10am-1pm) (Viva/Presentation:2pm-5pm)

Announcement of selected candidates: 15th July 2020

Last date for fees payment: 25th July 2020

Commencement of Semester: 25th July 2020

Eligibility

Candidates for admission to the PhD program should have a Masters degree or a professional degree declared equivalent to the Masters degree by the corresponding statutory regulatory body, with at least 55% marks in aggregate or its equivalent grade B in the UGC 7-point scale (or an equivalent grade in a point scale wherever grading system is followed).

For more details, please refer to our E-brochure.

The purpose is to develop and deliver contemporary and interdisciplinary programs to support participants pursuit of lifelong learning to be successful and excel over the years.

We aim to help corporate, educational institutions and individuals to increase their human potential by offering customized development programs. Keeping in mind the busy schedules of participants, the programs will be offered on-campus or at customers location or through an online platform.

For Corporate

  • Management Development Programs
  • Executive Development Programs
  • Certificate Programs

For Educational Institutes

  • Faculty Development Programs
  • Certificate Programs

For Professionals/Students

  • Executive Development Programs
  • Certificate Programs

Management Development Programs

These programs are aimed to address specific Executive Learning and/or Corporate Training requirements from individual organizations. In this format, courses are designed in collaboration with the client organization. The programs will cover wide areas such as Science, Engineering, Technology, Business, Environment, Architecture, and Education.

Executive Programs

To assist in the development of working professionals, we offer comprehensive programs that are tailored to suit specific needs and help personal and professional growth. The programs will be modular in nature and will be offered over a period of more than 12 months.

Faculty Development Programs

These programs are meant for faculties from high school, colleges, and post-graduate institutions. The objective is to contribute to the professional development of faculties by way of rigorous training in general management principles, pedagogical techniques, research methods and advanced topics in specialized areas.

Certificate Programs

The Certificate Program objective is to address the needs of professionals and individuals to develop specific skills to excel in their career. These programs are open to all practising/experienced managers, executives, entrepreneurs, students etc. The programs are delivered through a variety of teaching methods: case studies, simulations, role plays, closed group analysis and applied learning projects. The knowledge and insights gained as a result of such sessions have immediate applications for professional challenges.
