The International Cell @ NUV has been set up to support the University’s endeavors to internationalize the campus and curricula. The IC will assist in developing an extensive network with Universities and Institutions worldwide via international collaborations at many levels namely MOUs with universities abroad, joint academic programs, summer/winter internships, student/faculty exchange programs, distinguished visiting professors, lecture series, etc.

Student Internship at Egypt

Name : Mr. Rushi Vyas – TY BBA, School of Business and Law, Navrachana University, Vadodara.

Academic Program : International Internship at ABDA, Egypt (Coca-Cola Egypt’s Marketing Agency

Hosting University : American University of Cairo, Egypt

Date : Nov 29th 2019 to Jan 22nd, 2020

Educational Activity

In the internship, Mr. Rushi Vyas worked with a team of 4 people for Coca Cola, Egypt. The project involved the launch of new products. Mr. Rushi Vyas conducted market research to understand consumer trends; executed marketing plans to reach target audiences via appropriate channels. Liaised with external vendors to develop and execute events & campaigns.

Achievement : He got the best intern recognition and was awarded the certificate of excellence.

Program & Initiatives (2020-21)

  • Masters Admission
  • Online Preparatory Course Classes: IELTS
  • Group counselling sessions for study abroad ; Individual Counselling Sessions (As per request/need)
  • Parent Counselling
  • International University/College Admission Applications
  • Visa Guidance & Preparation ; Interview Preparation (wherever required)
  • Spot Admissions – As per international university promotion.
  • Scholarships
  • International Alumni Connect
  • Webinars with Learned International Personals / University Representatives
  • Online short certificate courses ; Summer/Winter Certificate Programs – 3 weeks / 4weeks
