Mr. Firdos Solanki

Assistant Professor, Business, SBL

Biography

Mr. Firdos Solanki joined Navrachana University in January, 2016 as an Assistant Professor.

Education

  • Firdos Solanki did his PGDM from IIM Kozhikode.
  • B.E. (Mechanical) – M.S.University, Vadodara.

Accomplishments

He has a total of six years of work experience across 4 companies L&T Technology Services, Tata Consultancy Services, Mahindra Logistics & Flipkart India Private Limited in that order.

His other qualifications include

  • Certified Supply Chain Professional from APICS – USA
  • Certified Six Sigma Green Belt from ASQ – USA
  • Lean Management Training from TMTC – Pune
  • ISO 9001:2008 Internal Auditor from Mahindra Logistics

He has had hands on experience in the areas of:

  • Reverse Engineering
  • Design & Development
  • Supply Chain Management
  • Operations Management
  • Logistics Management
  • Process Audit
  • Quality Management
